# TripVerse Driver Guide: Dual-Mode Operation

## Introduction

As a TripVerse driver, you can earn money in two ways:
1. **Car Rentals**: Multi-day bookings for intercity travel
2. **Ride-Hailing**: On-demand rides within your city

This guide explains how to set up and manage both services.

---

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- ✅ Verified driver account
- ✅ At least one active car registered
- ✅ Valid insurance and documents uploaded

## Setting Up Ride-Hailing

### Step 1: Enable Ride-Hailing for Your Car

1. Go to **Dashboard > My Cars**
2. Select the car you want to enable
3. Click **"Ride-Hailing Settings"**
4. Toggle **"Available for Ride-Hailing"** ON
5. Set your pricing:

| Setting | Description | Recommended Range |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Base Fare | Fixed starting fee | PKR 50-100 |
| Per KM Rate | Charge per kilometer | PKR 12-20 |
| Per Minute Rate | Charge per minute | PKR 1-3 |
| Minimum Fare | Lowest possible fare | PKR 80-150 |

### Step 2: Understand the Pricing Formula

Your earnings are calculated as:
```
Total Fare = Base Fare + (Distance × Per KM Rate) + (Duration × Per Minute Rate)
```

**Example:**
- Base Fare: PKR 50
- Distance: 10 km × PKR 15 = PKR 150
- Duration: 25 min × PKR 2 = PKR 50
- **Total: PKR 250**

During peak hours, a surge multiplier (1.2x-1.3x) is applied automatically.

### Step 3: Platform Fee

TripVerse takes a small percentage:
- **Ride-Hailing**: 15% platform fee
- **Rentals**: 5% platform fee

**Example**: PKR 250 fare → You earn PKR 212.50

## Mode Switching

### Understanding Modes

| Mode | Description | When to Use |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Offline** | Not accepting any bookings | Taking a break, car maintenance |
| **Ride-Hailing** | Accepting on-demand rides | Working within your city |
| **Rental** | Accepting multi-day rentals | Available for longer trips |

### How to Switch Modes

1. Open **Dashboard > Mode Control**
2. Select your desired mode
3. Click **"Switch Mode"**

### Mode Switching Rules

⚠️ **Important Restrictions:**

| Current Situation | Can Switch To |
|-------------------|---------------|
| No active bookings | Any mode |
| Active rental (future dates) | Cannot switch to Ride-Hailing |
| Active ride in progress | Cannot switch to Rental |
| Completed all bookings | Any mode |

**Example Scenario:**
- You have a rental booking for March 10-15
- On March 8, you CANNOT switch to ride-hailing mode
- On March 16 (after rental ends), you CAN switch freely

## Managing Ride Requests

### When a Request Arrives

1. You receive a notification with:
   - Pickup location
   - Dropoff location
   - Estimated fare
   - Distance & duration

2. You have **2 minutes** to respond

3. Options:
   - **Accept**: Start heading to pickup
   - **Reject**: Request goes to another driver

### Request Timeout

If you don't respond within 2 minutes:
- Request is automatically cancelled
- Customer is notified to try again
- Your acceptance rate may be affected

### Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Keep your app notifications ON
- Respond quickly to requests
- Maintain a high acceptance rate
- Update your availability status

❌ **Don't:**
- Accept requests you can't fulfill
- Cancel after accepting (affects rating)
- Drive in ride-hailing mode with rental bookings

## Dual-Mode Best Practices

### Strategy 1: Time-Based Switching

```
Weekday Schedule:
  Morning (7 AM - 10 AM): Ride-Hailing mode (high demand)
  Midday (10 AM - 5 PM): Check rental requests
  Evening (5 PM - 9 PM): Ride-Hailing mode (rush hour)
  Night: Offline
```

### Strategy 2: Booking-Based Switching

1. Keep **Rental mode** as default
2. Accept rentals for weekends/holidays
3. Switch to **Ride-Hailing** between rentals
4. Return to **Rental mode** when booking approaches

### Strategy 3: Seasonal Flexibility

| Season | Recommended Focus |
|--------|-------------------|
| Summer (May-Aug) | Rentals (vacation travel) |
| Eid / Holidays | Rentals (intercity travel) |
| Regular Weekdays | Ride-Hailing (daily commutes) |
| Rainy Season | Ride-Hailing (high demand) |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I do both rentals and ride-hailing?

Yes! But not simultaneously. Switch between modes based on your schedule.

### Why is my ride-hailing rate lower than expected?

Check your pricing settings. Also remember:
- Minimum fare applies to short trips
- Surge pricing varies by time of day
- 15% platform fee is deducted

### What happens if I miss a ride request?

The request times out after 2 minutes and the customer is notified. Try to respond quickly to maintain a good acceptance rate.

### Can I set different prices for different cars?

Yes! Each car can have independent ride-hailing pricing. Go to each car's settings individually.

### How do I maximize earnings?

1. **Peak Hours**: Work during morning (7-9 AM) and evening (5-7 PM) rush
2. **Weekends**: Use ride-hailing mode (1.2x surge)
3. **Rentals**: Accept long-distance intercity trips (higher margins)
4. **Mixed Strategy**: Ride-hailing on weekdays, rentals for weekends

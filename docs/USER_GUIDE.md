# TripVerse User Guide: Booking Cars

## Two Ways to Book

TripVerse offers two types of car services:

| Feature | Car Rental | Ride-Hailing |
|---------|------------|--------------|
| **Duration** | Multiple days | Single trip |
| **Use Case** | Intercity travel, vacations | Within-city rides |
| **Pricing** | Per day + distance | Per km + time |
| **Booking** | Plan ahead | Book instantly |

---

## When to Use Each

### Choose **Car Rental** When:
- 🚗 Traveling between cities (Karachi to Lahore)
- 📅 Need a car for multiple days
- 🏖️ Going on vacation or a road trip
- 💼 Business travel with multiple stops
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family trips requiring flexibility

### Choose **Ride-Hailing** When:
- 🏃 Need a quick ride within the city
- ✈️ Going to/from the airport
- 🎉 Night out (no need to drive back)
- 🛒 Running errands around town
- ⏰ Immediate transportation needed

---

## How to Book a Ride-Hailing Trip

### Step 1: Enter Locations
1. Open the TripVerse app
2. Enter your **pickup location**
3. Enter your **dropoff location**
4. The app will automatically detect this is a same-city ride

### Step 2: View Available Cars
- See cars available near you
- Each car shows:
  - Driver name & rating
  - Car model
  - Estimated fare
  - Estimated arrival time

### Step 3: Review Fare Breakdown
```
Base Fare:           PKR 50
Distance (10 km):    PKR 150
Time (25 min):       PKR 50
─────────────────────────────
Subtotal:            PKR 250
Surge (1.3x):        PKR 75
─────────────────────────────
Total:               PKR 325
```

### Step 4: Confirm Booking
1. Tap **"Book Now"**
2. Driver receives your request
3. Wait for confirmation (up to 2 minutes)
4. Once accepted, track driver's arrival

---

## How to Book a Car Rental

### Step 1: Enter Trip Details
1. Open TripVerse app
2. Enter **pickup city** (e.g., Karachi)
3. Enter **dropoff city** (e.g., Lahore)
4. Select **start date** and **end date**

### Step 2: Choose a Car
- Browse available cars for your dates
- Filter by:
  - Price range
  - Car type (sedan, SUV, etc.)
  - Transmission (automatic/manual)
  - Fuel type

### Step 3: Review Fare Breakdown
```
Rental (3 days × PKR 5,000): PKR 15,000
Distance (1,200 km):         PKR 6,000
─────────────────────────────────────
Subtotal:                    PKR 21,000
Platform Discount (5%):      -PKR 1,050
─────────────────────────────────────
Total:                       PKR 19,950
```

### Step 4: Confirm & Pay
1. Review booking details
2. Enter payment information
3. Tap **"Confirm Booking"**
4. Receive confirmation email

## Understanding Fare Transparency

### Ride-Hailing Fare Components

| Component | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Base Fare** | Starting cost for every ride |
| **Per KM Rate** | Charge for distance traveled |
| **Per Minute Rate** | Charge for time spent (traffic, waiting) |
| **Surge Pricing** | Higher rates during peak demand |
| **Minimum Fare** | Lowest possible charge (even for short trips) |

### When is Surge Pricing Applied?

| Time | Surge |
|------|-------|
| Weekday 7-9 AM | 1.3x (morning rush) |
| Weekday 5-7 PM | 1.3x (evening rush) |
| Weekends | 1.2x |
| Other times | No surge (1.0x) |

**Tip**: Book outside peak hours to save money!

### Rental Fare Components

| Component | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Daily Rate** | Cost per day of rental |
| **Distance Charge** | PKR 5 per kilometer |
| **Platform Fee** | 5% (lower than ride-hailing!) |

**Tip**: Rentals have a lower platform fee, making them more economical for longer trips!

## Cancellation Policy

### Ride-Hailing
- **Before driver accepts**: Free cancellation
- **After driver accepts**: PKR 50 cancellation fee
- **No-show**: Full fare charged

### Rentals
- **7+ days before**: Full refund
- **3-7 days before**: 50% refund
- **Less than 3 days**: No refund
- **No-show**: Full charge

## FAQs

### Why is my ride more expensive during rush hour?
Surge pricing helps ensure driver availability during high-demand times. Book outside peak hours for lower fares.

### Can I book a ride for someone else?
Yes! Enter their pickup location and share the booking details with them.

### What if my driver is late?
Contact your driver through the app. If they're significantly delayed, you can cancel with a full refund.

### How do I know if I should rent or hail?
- **Same city** → Ride-Hailing
- **Different city** → Rental
- **Multiple days** → Rental
- **Quick trip** → Ride-Hailing

### Is fuel included in rentals?
No, fuel costs are separate. The car will be provided with a full tank; please return it full.
